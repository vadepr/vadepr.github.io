Mental Health and Students


Disclaimer: We are mere students not experts. This is part of a project with the purpose of learning how to visualize data.

According to the World Health Organization (WHO), half of all mental health conditions start in the adolescence but most of them are undetected or untreated. Being depression the one leading causing illnesses and disability. There are a lot of factors that make adolescents vulnerable to mental health problems.


About the dataset used

Therefore, we wanted to make an exploratory research in this area using data of students from a French university published by Dryad.
This dataset was created to evaluate the health and lifestyle of undergraduates and asses risk factors associated with psychriatic symptoms. The data was recollected by the medical service of the unversitity by making the students undergo to a compulsory medical visit. This data includes the lifestyle of the students and prevalence to psychriatic symptoms. With this webpage we only want to inform about this mental health problem.
